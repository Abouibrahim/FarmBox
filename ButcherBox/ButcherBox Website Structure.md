## Key Features
### Subscription Model
- **Description**: Flexible subscription service
- **Box Sizes**: 6 products, 12 products
- **Customization**: Fully customizable selections
- **Frequency**: Flexible delivery schedule
- **Cancellation**: Skip or cancel anytime
### Sourcing
- **Beef**: 100% grass-fed and grass-finished from Australia or pasture-raised grain-finished from US
- **Poultry**: Organic free-range, humanely raised
- **Pork**: Crate-free, humanely raised
- **Seafood**: Wild-caught from American coastlines or responsibly farmed
- **Certifications**: B-Corp certified, third-party animal welfare certifications
### Pricing
- **Range**: $10.50-$15 per pound
- **Comparison**: Up to 15% savings vs. grocery stores
- **Shipping**: Free shipping included
- **Promotions**: Free protein offers for new subscribers
### Guarantees
- **Satisfaction**: 100% Satisfaction Guarantee
- **Freshness**: Frozen to lock in freshness with calculated dry ice
- **Delivery**: 1-3 day delivery window
